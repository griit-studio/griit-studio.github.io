about

Hello, this is Griit. We are the amalgamation of a photography and an architecture student who love experimenting with different ways of gifting weight and mass to digital spaces. Dancing between printed and digital matter, our practice-based research methodologies are born out of a deep love and reverence for obsolescent technologies and frivolous formats, coupled with an innate connection to digital spaces. We work closely with our clients to sensitively cultivate and celebrate the idiosyncrasies that make up their unique grit, so that their grittiness may permeate through whatever it is they wish to gift to the world. We have a comprehensive set of skills to match our sweeping, far-reaching interests; from fine art, design and drafting, to branding, copywriting and web development. Ultimately however, Griit can be summed up as a digital creative agency run by two creative problem solvers committed to the exploration and construction of new ideas, physical objects and digital spaces of enduring warmth.
